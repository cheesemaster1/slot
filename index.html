Core Mechanics

  • Heat Meter: builds with any win. At 100%, it upgrades symbols to wilds for the next spin.
  • Neon Wild: substitutes for any symbol and pays 5x if 3 land on the payline.
  • After Dark Scatter: land 3 scatter symbols anywhere to trigger 5 free spins.
  • Free Spins: wins during free spins add +2 spins if you land a win of 4x bet or more.
